HR Notification Email

Since this dashboard has no backend, notifications open as a pre-filled email in your mail client (mailto) rather than sending automatically. Set the HRBP/HR Head inbox here once — it's remembered on this browser.

Step 1 — Stores Requiring Action (L&D)

Latest audit per store, filtered by threshold. Starting a process notifies the store's RM and ROM by email and creates a tracked case.

Step 2 — Awaiting ROM Submission

ROM (or RM) enters the defaulting employee and Store Director details, then submits — this notifies HR.

Step 3 — Awaiting HR Action

HRBP / HR Head selects the action per policy and closes the process.
